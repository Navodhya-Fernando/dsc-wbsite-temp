Our Journey

2020 - Foundation

DSC was established with a vision to democratize data science education and foster innovation among students.

2021 - Growth Phase

Membership grew to 150+ students. Launched first annual NextGen Fest with 500+ participants.

2022 - Recognition

Published first research paper. Won innovation award for best student organization at NIBM.

2023 - Digital Transformation

Launched digital platform, introduced online learning resources, and expanded event portfolio.

2024 - Industry Partnership

Established partnerships with leading tech companies and research institutions for collaborative projects.

2025 - Scale & Impact

Reached 500+ members, published 10+ research papers, developed 15+ innovative tools.
